About Us

Good Day! We appreciate you for giving the time to view our site. So, this is a collaborative portofolio of BSIT students specializing in Web and Mobile Applications in FEU Institute of Technology.

This are projects created by the bewildering Catherine Joy Chan, the amazing John Lerick Estacio and lastly, the prodigious Camille Mendoza. They excel not only in their field but also in other forms that require such creativity.

It has been a pleasure for giving us the privilege of your time to view our collective works. Till next time and for more projects to come. We would like to Thank you from the bottom of our hearts and God Bless us all!
